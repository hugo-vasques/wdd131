Cajamarca, Peru

Cajamarca, located in the northern highlands of Peru, is a historic city where the Incas and the Spanish met. It is known for its impressive Andean landscapes, dairy farming, colorful Carnival celebrations, and iconic sites such as Atahualpa's Ransom Room, the Otuzco Windows, and its hot springs.
