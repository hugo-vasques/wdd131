Peru got taste!

Site Name

Peru got taste! It was chosen because it reflects the cultural and culinary richness of the country.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to showcase typical Peruvian dishes, their history, and ingredients, and promote gastronomic tourism in each region.

Scenarios

  • What typical dishes should I try if I travel to the coast, mountains, or jungle of Peru?
  • What is the history and origin of ceviche?

Color Schema

The selected colors represent cultural identity:

  • Light Red (#CC010A): Title, header and footer.
  • Light Green (#189E01): Accents and details.
  • White (#FFFFFF):Main background for better contrast.

Typography

Selected fonts:

  • Funnel Sans - For the titles.
  • Archivo Narrow - Paragraphs and general text.
